Achieve

A social skills program for ages 14-24.

Achieve offers 10-12 engaging experiences yearly, like bowling, BBQs, and a "Night at the Museum" overnight, helping neurodivergent teens and young adults build confidence, communication, and teamwork skills.

In partnership with Buena Vista Recreation Department, Achieve creates inclusive opportunities to boost mental health and independence.

To join the Achieve Social Program, please complete a one-time registration by submitting the following forms:

  • registration form
  • transportation release
  • media release
Once you're registered for the program, you’ll need to sign up for each individual event through BV Rec as they are scheduled.
